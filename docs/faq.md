# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q: Does EduQuick work on mobile?

- **iOS:** Yes, follow the iOS Safari instructions
- **Android:** Limited support, bookmarklet must be pasted manually every time
  {: .warning }
    > Android support is strongly discouraged.

---

## Q: Is EduQuick safe?

Yes, EduQuick only reads quizzes in your browser and does not store your login info.

---

## Q: What browsers are supported?

- Chrome & Chromium-based (Brave, Opera, Vivaldi)
- Edge
- Safari (iOS only)
- Others may work using manual bookmarklet

---

## Q: What to do if the bookmarklet doesn’t run?

- Ensure you are logged in to Educake
- Follow the [installation instructions](/installation){: .btn .btn-purple}
- Check console for errors if using a desktop browser
