# Using EduQuick

EduQuick is a bookmarklet for **[Educake](https://www.educake.co.uk){:target="\_blank" rel="noopener"}** that helps you save time on repetitive quizzes by collecting answers before you start and optionally auto-completing them.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Log in to Educake

Open [Educake](https://my.educake.co.uk/my-educake/){:target="\_blank" rel="noopener"} and sign in to your student account.

### 2. Verify Installation

Ensure your bookmarklet is correctly set up in your browser's bookmarks bar. If you haven't installed it yet, follow the guide below:

[Installation Guide](/installation){: .btn .btn-purple}

### 3. Launch the tool

Click **EduQuick** from your bookmarks bar. An interface will appear on the page.

### 4. Complete your quiz

Open the specific quiz you want to work on. EduQuick will automatically begin collecting answers as you progress. You can also enable **auto-answer** mode within the interface to speed up the process.

---

## Best Practices

To ensure the best experience and maintain a natural progress profile, keep the following tips in mind:

{: .note }

> **Pacing:** Be mindful of your completion time. Teachers can see how long it takes to finish a quiz; finishing too quickly may look suspicious.

{: .note }

> **Mobile Users:** If you are on a phone or tablet, rotate your device to **landscape mode** and zoom out fully to see the entire interface.

{: .warning }
