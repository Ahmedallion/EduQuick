# Using EduQuick

EduQuick is a bookmarklet that collects answers and optionally auto-completes quizzes on **Educake**.

---

## Steps to Use

1. Log in to [Educake](https://www.educake.co.uk){:target="\_blank" rel="noopener"}
2. Ensure your bookmarklet is installed ([Installation](/installation){: .btn .btn-purple})
3. Open the quiz you want to complete
4. Click **EduQuick** from your bookmarks bar
5. Watch EduQuick automatically collect answers
6. Optionally enable **auto-complete** mode

---

## Tips

{: .note }

> Be mindful of time, as teachers can see how long it takes you to complete the quiz.

{: .note }

> On mobile, turn your device horizontal and zoom out as much as possible.
